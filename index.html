Powerful Features

Programmable Controllers

Fully customizable sliders, buttons, and bidimensional pads for complete expressive control over your MIDI parameters. Design your perfect control surface.

Dual Connectivity

Connect via USB (IDAM) for zero-latency or Wi-Fi for maximum flexibility. Seamlessly integrates with Mac and professional DAWs.

Relative Cursor Mode

Avoid unwanted jumps with relative mode, perfect for live performances without needing to look at the screen. Professional-grade precision.

Unique Decay System

Exclusive automatic return-to-rest functionality, ideal for expressive controls like modulation and dynamics. Spring-loaded behavior.

Adaptive Interface

Automatically adapts to any iOS screen size, from iPhone to iPad, in any orientation. Optimal ergonomics for every device.

Custom Presets

Save and manage configurations for different musical projects and instrument libraries. Your setups, your way.
